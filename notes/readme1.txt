CPU- CPU or the Central Processing Unit is like the brain of computer as it does the most processing or thinking in a computer.

RAM- The RAM or the Random Access Memory acts like a scratch pad temporaily storing files.

HDD- The HDD or Hard Disk Drive is what stores information or computer software for later on usage even after you shut down your pc or computer.
